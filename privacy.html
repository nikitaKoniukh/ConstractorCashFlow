Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

KablanPro does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with us or any third parties. All your data stays on your device and your personal iCloud account.

Overview

KablanPro ("the app") is developed by Nikita Koniukh ("we", "us", "our"). This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Financial data you enter into the app
  • Device identifiers or usage analytics
  • Location data
  • Contact information

iCloud Sync

KablanPro uses Apple's CloudKit framework to sync your data across your own devices. Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is only accessible by you. We have no access to this data at any time.

In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit 2. We do not process or store any payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles purchase data.

Local Notifications

The app may send local push notifications for invoice reminders and budget alerts. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party SDKs

KablanPro does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

KablanPro is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
